## 🌡️ July Weather Advisory
July is Japan's hottest, most humid month (29-32°C/84-90°F with 78-83% humidity). The rainy season typically ends mid-July, so expect some rain early in your trip. **Plan indoor activities during peak heat (11am-4pm)** and save outdoor sightseeing for early morning or evening.
